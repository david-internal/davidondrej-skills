# YouTube Polls

Goal: understand a YouTube audience by learning about their needs, preferences, and interests. Each poll should improve general audience understanding.

## Rules

- One clear question + exactly four options.
- Each poll stands alone. Never assume voters saw or voted in any other poll — no follow-ups that depend on prior polls.
- Options must be very clear, 3–6 words max.
- Each poll is its own independent research question. Never design a poll as a follow-up to, or a drill-down into, a previous poll's result. Treat every poll as if it's the first and only question asked. Generate questions from scratch against general audience research goals — not from what was last asked.
